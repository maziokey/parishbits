Rev. Fr. Moses Jimbili - Parish Priest

St Kevin's Catholic Church, Jikwoyi

St Kevin's Catholic Church, Jikwoyi is a community of Catholic Faithful drawn from Jikwoyi and parts of Karu, Mararaba and Orozo. The Over 2000 member parish is pastored by Rev. Fr. Moses Jimbili (Parish Priest) and Rev. Fr. Sabastian Musa (Associate Priest), under the authority of His Grace, Most Rev. Dr. Ignatius Ayau Kaigama, Archbishop of the Metropolitan See of Abuja Ecclesiastical Province.

Parishioners of St. Kevin's, known for their zeal and cheerful disposition, are predominantly traders, civil servants and peasants.
